Our Dairy Process

From Rearing to Testing: Our Dairy Products For Healthy Living

Rearing

The cows are raised and taken care of, ensuring they have a healthy diet and living conditions.

Milking

Cows are milked using machines that mimic the natural sucking action of a calf. This is usually done twice a day.

Storing

The milk is collected and stored in refrigerated vats to keep it fresh.

Testing

The milk is tested for quality and safety. It considers the purity, quality, protein, allergenicity, and microbiological contamination.
